User Guide

Learn how to use the property valuation calculator

1

Select Sector Number

Use the dropdown menu to select the appropriate sector number for the property you are evaluating. Each sector represents a predefined geographical division, and the rates may vary depending on the sector selected.

2

Carpet Area

Enter the carpet area of the property in square meters (Sq. Mtr.) as measured or specified in property documents (Index II). Ensure accurate measurement to avoid discrepancies in valuation.

3

Terrace / Open Balcony Area

In cases where the terrace or open balcony area is explicitly specified as a separate entry in the registered property document (Index II), the area must be entered in square meters (Sq. Mtr.).

If such an area is not distinctly mentioned in the Index II, kindly enter 0 in this field.

4

Depreciation / Ghasara

Depreciation or "Ghasara" accounts for the age of the property and the reduction in its value over time due to wear and tear or obsolescence. Enter the number of years since the property was constructed.

Example:

For a building that received OC in year 2014:
Current Year - 2014 = 2024 - 2014 = 10
Therefore, Enter "10" in this field.

5

Floor Number

Enter the numerical value of the floor number.

Example:

ground floor = 0, first floor = 1, second floor = 2, and so on.
